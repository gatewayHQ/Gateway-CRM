Our Method

A rigorous, four-stage valuation

01

Financial Underwriting

We normalize your T-12 and rent roll, reconstruct in-place NOI, and stress-test income and expense assumptions against market benchmarks.

02

Market Comparables

We assemble recent closed sales and active listings in your immediate submarket, adjusted for vintage, unit mix, condition, and location quality.

03

Cap-Rate & Capital Markets

We apply current market cap rates and financing conditions — including assumable-debt upside — to triangulate a defensible value range, not a single guess.

04

Physical & Value-Add Assessment

We evaluate condition, deferred maintenance, and repositioning potential — RUBS, unit renovations, and operational efficiencies — to quantify latent upside.
